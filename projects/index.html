ARKEM and Unreal Engine production work

Exovision Games is centered on ARKEM and a focused set of Unreal Engine tools, assets and support work for developers.

ARKEM Overview

A medieval single-player strategy RPG

ARKEM combines modular castle building, settlement management, real-time strategy decisions and third-person exploration in a medieval-inspired world.

ARKEM is currently in development. A public demo has been released, the game is available to wishlist on Steam, and the full release date is to be announced.

Gameplay pillars

ARKEM

Build

Modular castle construction and settlement layout as core long-term play.

Rule

Settlement management, resources and growth decisions over time.

Defend

Troops, threats and castle defense tied to your strategic choices.

Explore

Third-person movement, quests, NPCs and world exploration.

What makes ARKEM distinct

What makes ARKEM distinct

ARKEM switches between wider strategic control and direct third-person presence, with castle and settlement development at the center of play. It combines RTS, RPG and city-building ideas without claiming a finished full release before it is ready.
