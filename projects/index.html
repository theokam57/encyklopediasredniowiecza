ARKEM

Experience the medieval era

ARKEM is our flagship original project: a blend of real-time strategy and third-person RPG action set in a hand-crafted medieval world.

The project is being built in Unreal Engine with a focus on tactical command, grounded atmosphere, and the feeling of stepping from a wider campaign into the action yourself.
